## "Viewpoint" Sleep Cycle
Sleep time is not simply continuous. Adult humans sleep in a cycle of **90 minutes (1.5 hours)**.
In a cycle, people's sleep state is light sleep, then gradually enters deep sleep, and finally returns to light sleep**.
![image](https://imgs.zhubai.love/aa012443af8d4bc1b726f4390751c03d.png)
That is to say, a person is in the deepest sleep state during the 45th minute of falling asleep, returns to light sleep when sleeping for 90 minutes, and repeats this process in the next 90-minute cycle.
It is easier to wake up from a light sleep state, and you will not feel particularly sleepy when you wake up at this time;
It’s terrible to be awakened from a deep sleep state. After waking up, you still feel sleepy and have the urge to wake up😡.
### **R90 sleep method**
One sleep lasts for just the right number of integer cycles.
- For example, if the sleep time is 00:00 in the evening and the wake-up time is 07:30 in the morning, this sleep will be exactly 5 integer cycles.
- The best daytime nap duration is 20 to 30 minutes
