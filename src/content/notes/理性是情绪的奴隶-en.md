#### **1. Identity, social ethics**
**Humans are social animals, and reason is hidden in "identity"**.
Identity depends on the attributes of the small environment. You are a child at home, a student at school, and an employee at the company. These are all attributes of the social environment.
We long to integrate into the group, so we need **"identity"**
In society, people are subject to "social situations", and the constraints are a set of rules and cultural systems, which we call ethics.
People will restrict what they want to do because of their status and social ethics, which will lead to physical and mental unhealthy.
Let’s give an easy-to-understand example: when playing Werewolf, why the level of good guy cards and werewolf cards are completely different.
Because the hint of the "identity" of good guys and bad guys will lead to unnatural states and distortion of expression.
We like to watch movies and play social platforms because movies and social platforms have different identity designs or weaken identities.
The social ethics system has less constraints, so a person who seems normal can become a troll on social networks. Obviously timid people like to watch horror movies, because after their "identity" is blurred, they start to let themselves go.
