## "Plan" is procrastination.
Habits are everything, they govern all behaviors and ways of thinking.
Some habits are developed since childhood, such as the way to go to the toilet, when to cry, laugh, etc. Over time, some behaviors become part of ourselves.
These habits are constantly running in the subconscious mind of the brain. Because of the need to control [stress](https://yeshu.zhubai.love/posts/2091453237944827904) and mood swings from time to time, the prefrontal cortex of the brain will only leave a little energy for new habits.
I had never successfully exercised consistently before. Later, I formulated a plan of 20 dumbbell rows and 20 seconds of plank support each time, which would not put too much burden on the body and must be adhered to every day. Over time, I felt some changes in my body.
**Persisting in exercising allows me to see the power of habits. **
Exercising for three months can help you get in good shape, but it won't keep you in good shape. If you cannot develop a habit, sooner or later you will be replaced by a new habit, such as a simpler habit.
**I summarized four common misunderstandings:**
**1. Lazy and escaping**
> How to use "one minute" to develop good habits?
> 4 ultimate laws of self-discipline and 12 practical tips to save procrastination!
> Teach you to develop good habits from 0-1 in four simple steps!
Seeing these titles is really exciting. But the fact is that only people who want to be self-disciplined and develop good habits will click to watch the video, but the creators take advantage of the "lazy" habits among viewers to attract clicks to watch.
Of course, this is not to deny that the views and methods described in these videos are invalid, but that some people who are self-disciplined simply cannot understand it and cannot correctly teach viewers who are still worried about self-discipline.
Right, Jay Chou can't teach another Jay Chou anyway, but maybe a music teacher can.
Don't be lazy. Habits can only be formed step by step. Most of those who tell you how to form a habit with a few tips are selling second-hand knowledge. A few are talented people. Only a few are good-hearted people who want to use the title to attract you and really want to teach you how to form a habit step by step.
Every time we say, “I’m going to learn something new” or “I’m going to develop a habit,” we are actually cringing. **
What we are actually saying is: "Give me some more time, I'm not ready yet." **
To say "plan" is to procrastinate and to make excuses for yourself. However, even if you don't take action, at least tell yourself truthfully: "Although I said I wanted to do it, I didn't really want to do it. If I really wanted to, I would have done it a long time ago."
> The most difficult thing about taking a bath in winter is not the bathing itself, but the tiptoeing and caution before taking a bath.
> If you want to do it, 99% of the time the best time is now.
One way to avoid avoidance is to make it known to everyone around you. You can say to everyone you know: "I think, I promise."
**2. Strategic delay zone**
> Well, I’m quite busy at work today, so let’s get busy with work first!
> Well, I finally finished my work, let’s forget it this time!
> Well, it doesn't matter if you don't do it today.
Yes, there are always more important things than habits. The meaning of strategic procrastination is to deal with the things that need to be dealt with first and arrange the important things later.
Sounds reasonable, right? Yes, but have you ever thought that not everyone knows what is really important, or that not everyone is good at judging whether something is important.
Solution: Turn the habit into a small habit that is very easy to operate. Count and clock in every day, and have proof of continuous clocking in. It is best to share the clocking in record.
**3. Local optimum**
> I have been exercising hard for a week, and my body shape has improved a lot!
What does this sentence mean? This means that this is an effective method, because this habit does improve your posture.
But the solution to a problem should not be partial. If this habit cannot be maintained for a long time, this exercise method can only be a local optimum.
Maybe it was achieved because I was not busy during this period and did a week of high-load exercise. **Local Optimal** refers to the hardest and easiest method among the correct methods. The **global optimal** refers to the best one among all methods, which can be persisted for a long time.
**4. Underestimating effectiveness and lacking patience**
>Hold on a little longer!
Yes, most habits die in the darkness before dawn.
