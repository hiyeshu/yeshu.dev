## Why can’t we live well?
### 🤬 Behind the buzzwords
In your corner of the Internet in 2022, you will find that language has evolved to a new stage. YYDS, Juejuezi, Dianzhongdian, Bengbuzhu, 💉8️⃣❌, more and more words are beginning to be incomprehensible.
Buzzwords are a new symbol system based on language. The main feature is that it saves effort and can express more meanings through abstract words.
This is an inflation of the language.
**"The more a word is used, the weaker its original meaning becomes, thus requiring stronger vocabulary"**
### 🔮 Moore’s Law of All Things
OpenAI CEO Sam Altman mentioned a concept, Moore's Law for Everything. **
The general content is as follows:
- We know there are two ways to live a good life: make everyone rich, or buy things cheaper.
- Over the past few decades, the cost of television, computers, and entertainment in the United States has fallen, but other costs have risen dramatically, most notably the cost of housing, health care, and higher education. If these costs continue to soar, wealth redistribution alone will not work.
- Artificial intelligence can reduce the cost of goods and services because labor is a driving cost at many supply chain levels.
- AI doctors can diagnose health problems better than any human doctor, and AI teachers can accurately locate and explain problems that students don’t understand.
- "Moore's Law of Everything" is a clarion call for a generation that can't afford the things they want. Imagine a world in which in the next few decades, the price of everything, including housing, education, food, clothing, etc., will be halved every two years. Although it sounds like a fantasy, technology can indeed achieve this goal for us.
### Summary
Buzzwords are constantly changing, which means our everyday language is becoming simpler and simpler.
"🐑" is easier to understand than "I am infected with COVID-19".
At the same time, the cost of having a good life is getting lower and lower, but because we are used to a good life, we need more and better lifestyles.
**This is why we cannot live well. **
