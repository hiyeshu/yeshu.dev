## Use endorphins to quickly enter the state
Endorphins, also known as endorphins, are a hormone in the human body. Its direct effect is to produce pain relief, make people feel physically and mentally happy, and relieve stress. **
The effect of endorphins is very close to that of another drug, morphine, so it is also called a natural analgesic.
The areas where the human body produces the most endorphins and the areas most affected by endorphins are areas related to learning and memory. Therefore, endorphins can improve learning efficiency and deepen memory.
### Methods to promote endorphin secretion
1. Only when the body exercises for more than 30 minutes, especially under the influence of heart rate, can the body enter a state of excessive consumption of blood sugar, protect and maintain body balance, maintain body health, and suppress pain, a large amount of endorphins will be produced (this is very reflected in the work efficiency of fitness after get off work last week)
2. Mountain climbing, meditation, sitting still, yoga, etc. will also increase the secretion of endorphins (the sense of achievement brought by control)
