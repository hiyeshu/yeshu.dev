## What does swimming teach me?
After the Cambrian explosion, our ancestors crawled out of the sea. Water should be the most primitive environment for human beings
### **From playing in the water to swimming**
- Playing in the water only depends on whether you have fun or not, but swimming depends on whether you swim well or not.
- Swimming well is filled with affirmation and praise. You can understand that "swimming well" is everywhere, and these "swimming well" make you dare not stop for a moment, for fear of falling behind. "Swimming well" will be endorsed and praised by others. So there are two types of people in swimming: "poor swimmers" and "hate swimmers"
- In the swimming lane, it is possible to start swimming at any time without natural waves like in the sea. So it feels strange to park in the pool for ten minutes, and it feels a bit strange to sit on the edge of the pool.
- The swimming lane has gradually developed into a "track". It seems that the only way to be normal in the swimming lane is to practice constantly.
### From the sea to a swimming road
- The lane is filled with the sound of "swim well", which grabs your attention and keeps you in a busy state of excitement, losing the ability to stop.
- Swimming lanes, everyone seems to only care about swimming by themselves, and communication and connection are reduced in the environment. Oh, it seems like only the swimming instructor is talking
- The feedback in the water is very immediate. When you move, the water will always react to you immediately and respond to every movement. This constant feedback is what makes the whole body tired afterwards.
### So
- Without the relaxation and rest at the beach, we come to the swimming pool just to swim, just to "swim well". What other people do, most likely has nothing to do with me. (This may be the reason why accidents rarely happen on the beach, but accidents often occur in swimming pools)
- Lost your own swimming movements, because there is more than one swimming style to "swim well", such as "*synchronized swimming*"
- Continuous swimming makes our emotions dilute. We will not be angry at the people in the next lane who create big waves, but become unhappy. Think about the repressive mood in the company now. It will not make you flip the table in anger, but it will consume you all the time, but it will also prevent you from completely mustering up the courage to leave.
-Resist yourself from swimming poorly. In fact, when you are not swimming well, it just lets you know that you need to practice
- Lost real friends, in the swimming pool, everyone formed a group of applauding audience. "Have a good swim" catchphrase "Are you working overtime today?"
- Having lost your true self, only "swimming well" in the swimming lane has value. Swimming around all the time, "swimming poorly" without the time and attention to practice properly, and it is difficult to achieve the so-called "swimming well", so I become increasingly depressed and tired, drowning in self-pity, and finally "hate swimming"
**People who can’t swim don’t really know how to swim, but they don’t know why they swim. **
> The meaning of swimming is: either go to different beaches to swim, or swim in a swimming pool and swim in your own style
