People need to work, but they actually need "rest".
## **Focus**
Our ability to focus is getting worse. Why?
- In fact, people's own concentration ability is limited. It is changes in the environment that make concentration ability seem to get worse.
- As a herd animal instinct, attention is swallowed up by the instant feedback of social media (likes, replies)
- I think things at the moment are meaningless, so I want to escape
### **The meaning of concentration (1) Rest**
Most of the energy in the brain is consumed in the DMN (default neural network), which is characterized by being in a daze and doing nothing.
If a person's state is simply divided into three categories:
- Nothing to do
- Do/Think complex things
- do/think simple things
**DMN occurs in the first two categories, but not in the third category. **
Research, including by psychologists, also shows that the **DMN is closed when people perform purposeful external tasks. **
The so-called purposeful external work is most representative of focusing on simple mechanical activities.
Concentration is also a non-DMN, and its energy consumption is 5% of the daze state.
Summary: **Focus can rest the brain better than being in a daze. **
### **The meaning of concentration (2) No anxiety**
Unfortunately, DMN involves areas of the brain that are also responsible for feeling emotions and the ability to empathize, which are highly overlapping.
So in trance mode, the characteristics of the brain are:
- Negativism (do I look good in this)?
- Mind reading (how people around me see me)
- Reflect on the past and plan for the future (well, I have to do the laundry tomorrow)
All of this is actually related to the survival needs of human beings in ancient times. The "incompatibility" between the lifestyle of modern society and this daze mode is the cause of mental internal friction. **
Therefore, when you use Douyin, you have turned on the DMN mode. You will only become more tired and exhausted as you use TikTok.
Instead, you may focus on reading a long novel. The pictures and concepts in the text require your concentration to be constructed. This is a good way to resist anxiety and take a rest.
