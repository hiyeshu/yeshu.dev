### **Reasons for not being able to create the life you want**
1. I dare not imagine what kind of life I want.
2. What you want is an unthinking, implanted idea.
3. Want the means, not the ends
> I dare not imagine what kind of life I want
I have lacked the freedom of choice since I was a child, so that the ability to "make choices" has not been cultivated. I have forgotten that I can ask myself "What do I really want?", and I lack the imagination for the life I want to live.
But in fact, it is possible to move from concept to reality. After clarifying the "purpose" and "current situation", structural tension will be generated and you will be filled with inner drive. When you are clear about what you want, you can also pay attention to more clues related to it in your life.
> What you want is an unthinking, implanted thought
They want to travel around the world, work freelance, etc., but they have never taken the step forward because these are not what they really want in their hearts, but they just want it because they see others doing well and are quite envious.
Or you are forced to tell yourself because of your identity, status, etc., that you have this.
The result may be that due to lack of motivation and insufficient research, either you will not start, or you will be disappointed after you go.
> Want the means, not the end
"I want to open a flower shop." This is actually a "means". Maybe your "purpose" is "I want to live in an environment full of flowers and plants, and meet some people who like flowers and plants", or "I want to use my artistic aesthetic in flower arrangement, and everyone likes to come to buy flowers from me."
Then the purpose of the former may be "to live in an environment with flowers and plants, and to meet some people who like flowers and plants."
The purpose of the latter may be "I hope my artistic aesthetic can help everyone and at the same time have a certain influence."
Although opening a flower shop seems to be a reasonable path, if you don't like things like business promotion, these will affect the survival of a flower shop.
Therefore, it is important to distinguish between "means" and "end".
If you don't know where you want to go, most people will stand in your way.
If you know where you want to go, most people can help you.
